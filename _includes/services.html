Features

We help our customers accept electronic payments directly, with or without a local bank.

Eliminate Merchant Fees

If you can accept credit cards, but it takes a significant part of your profit for merchant fees, interchange fees, chargebacks and monthly minimums. Wampum Register© allows you eliminate these expenses.

Speed

You control your funds throughout the transaction and can spend it in as soon as 45 minutes.

Bankless Transactions

If you do not or can not have a local bank, you are not able to accept checks or credit cards. With Wampum Register© you can accept electronic payments easily. You can also make electronic payments to your vendors and employees.

Prepayments / Delivery

You can allow your customers to order and pay for items in advance, allowing for a quick, efficient purchase experience or product delivery.

Security

Cash-heavy businesses pay as much as 20% of their revenue to secure cash. Wampum Register© allows you to lower that by as much as 99%.

Cryptocurrency

If you find that most or all of your sales are cash for whatever reason, using Wampum Register© is a great option for your business. Cash can be simple, but too much of it makes you a target for crime. Safely inventorying and using cash can also be very expensive.
