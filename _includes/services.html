Features

We help our customers accept electronic payments directly and without the requirement of a local bank.

Cryptocurrency

If you find that most or all of your sales are cash for whatever reason, using Wampum Register© is a great option for your business. Cash can be simple, but too much of it makes you a target for crime. Safely inventorying and using cash can also be very expensive.

Bankless Transactions

If you do not or can not have a local bank, you may not be able to accept checks or credit cards. With Wampum Register© you can accept electronic payments easily. You can also make electronic payments to your vendors and/or employees.

Prepayments / Delivery

You can allow your customers to order and pay for items in advance, allowing for a quick, efficient purchase experience or product delivery.

Security

Cash-heavy business pay as much as 20% to secure cash while it is being held and transferred. Wampum Register© allows you to lower that by as much as 99%.

Eliminate Merchant Fees

If you are able to accept credit cards, but it takes a significant part of your profit for merchant fees, interchange fees, chargebacks and monthly minimums. Wampum Register© allows you eliminate chargebacks and eliminate merchant fees.
